1. Project Title and Description

Title: Career Advancement Preparation

The project I chose is to design and execute a Professional Career Advancement plan aimed at securing an entry level IT helpdesk position. This project will focus on completing industry recognized certificates, updating and adding value to my resume and enhanceing my LinkedIn profile. I also plan to create a portfolio and run a job search which will help increase my networking skills.

2. Mission, Vision and Strategy

Mission: Execute a structured career plan, complete certificates, upgrade my resume and create a portfolio and network which will help me find jobs all with the end goal of supporting my career in IT.

Vision: Prepare myself to be a strong candidate for an entry level IT role by the end of the semester.

Strategy: Follow a consistent well structured plan ensuring my deliverables are completed on time, create a portfolio that highlights my expertise, study for and complete industry wide accepted certificates.

3. Business Case

This project will help me grow professionally by strengthening my networking skills and work on my communication skills by expressing my ideas clearly in both written and presentation formats. These skills will help me in my professional career by opening new job opportunities in IT field.

On a personal level this project will make me more marketable to employers and also strengthen my resume and abilities since I will be learning new skills with every new certificate I gain.

4. High Level Scope

In Scope for Semester Long Project

  • Complete industry recognized certificates
  • Update and refine my resume while also adding new details
  • Create a professional portfolio that highlights my skills and abilities
  • Apply to 10 IT entry level helpdesk job positions
  • Update my LinkedIn account to boost my online presence

Roadmap Items up to 6 Months After Semester Ends

  • Continue to update my resume and adding value to it
  • Continue to apply for new possitions and internships to gain more experience
  • Continue to network in person and online via LinkedIn
  • Present my finished portfolio to employers

Truly Out of Scope

  • Complete high level certificates that have demanding requirements
  • Guaranteed job offers
  • Long term maintenance on all my deliverables
  • Get hired for positions above entry level

6. Project Methodology Explanation

For my methodology I chose hybrid iterative for my project. One reason I chose this method is because the structure and deadlines are fixed. This is beneficial for me because it keeps my work paced at a steady rate, which improves quality and prevents rushing everything at the end.

Another reason this methodology helps is the lower risk of misalignment with project requirements because regular feedback sessions make sure my work stays clear and aligned with the rubric. Finally, this approach is well balanced and includes everything I need to produce quality work. The schedule is not too fast paced, but it is consistent.

8. Project Tool Selection Explanation

Choose Your Tool. After reviewing Asana, Trello, and Microsoft Project, I have decided to use Trello for my project management tool because it best fits my needs and supports the structure of this project. Trello is easy to work with and provides features that help me visually map my hybrid methodology.

Trello is also free, and I have used it on previous projects, so the learning curve is minimal. Microsoft Project was another strong option, but its more complex features are beyond what I need right now. I also reviewed Asana and liked that it's free, but since I have more experience with Trello, I chose it as my primary tool and would consider Asana as a second option.
